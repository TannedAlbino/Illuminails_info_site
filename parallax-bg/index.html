welcome to Illuminails

Illuminails are flexible OLED screen fingernails that display colors, images, and designs from your phone or tablet.

Fashion technology for your fingernails.

color choices

Every nail has access to every color in your imagination to match your style at the push of button.

save favorites

Save your favorite nail colors, images, and designs to your nail configuration sets.

Creative content

A nail design marketplace where you can upload your own designs and download others great designs, right to your fingertips.

Responsive design

Communication from your phone or tablet to your fingernails as often as you feel you need a change in style.

Benefit of the App

The app allows you to create as many sets of nails in whatever colors you choose for certain outfits, designs for occasions, colors for the time of day, and all available on and offline.

  • Stunning flexibility

    You control what each nail looks like at any time, anywhere.

  • Your Designs

    You have ideas for designs. Create them, use them, upload them to the marketplace and sell them.

  • Shop for styles

    The marketplace allows you to browse and purchase any design for you to use as often as it matches you.

  • Ease of use

    The app is designed to be quick and simple to use intuitively to go from decision to exhibiting your style.

  • unlimited storage

    Your color palette, designs, and nail sets are saved locally and in the cloud. You will never lose any of the selections you have made.

  • image designs

    Take your favorite photos and use them as your newest style. Take a photo of your outfit and the app will create a color palette to match.
